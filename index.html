About

Hi, I'm Aref.

Currently, I am studying Master of Computer Science at Shahid Beheshti University. I also work as a Tech Lead of CafeBazaar in Application-Search Team. CafeBazaar is a software company producing an app store for Android platform; hosting thousand of Iranian apps. In CafeBazaar, I am working both as a backend developer and technical leader. As a developer, we are developing a highly available system in Linux via Python to provide lists of applications for users search query. At the moment we have more than two million queries daily. As a Tech Lead, I have to consult my team members about system architecture and code reliability. Also, using the Agile principles I am working toward coordinating my team to work flawlessly together.

Previously, I was Team Leader at NAO Bipedal Laboratory (aka MRL-SPL Team) which was supervised under Qazvin Azad University. In NAO-Lab, we were working with NAO bipedal robots in order to participate in RoboCup competitions. The goal was to play soccer. I was in charge of the vision system. Throughout the years, we start using Neural Network approaches for detecting objects since a more reliable system was required.

Also, I worked in a startup group named NAB. Which was under supervision of Software Research Center (Currently known as SYNTECH CENTER), In NAB we were working on 3D model reconstruction of 2D images (either photo or video); We have developed photogrammetry tools to achieve this goal and put it online at NAB web-page.

Beside my profession, I am really interested in Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Machine Image Understanding.
